AI Cognitive Offload Slider

This slider illustrates how Generative AI can be used to varying degrees for a single assignment: a presentation on Cumberland Gap National Historical Park. In this scenario, students are tasked with researching and presenting on the park's significance, history, and geography. Depending on the learning goals of the course or assignment, different levels of AI involvement may be appropriate. Use the slider below to explore five levels of increasing cognitive offload to AI—from fully student-generated work to near-complete AI generation.

Level 1 – Fully Human-Crafted
Student does all research, writing, and presentation design without AI.
